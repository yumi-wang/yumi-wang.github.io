Bootcamp Learning Plan

30 September 2022


What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

My goal is to be a confident programmer who offers support to others. I would like to take away the skills gained at dev academy as my asset to be a part of the tech field and land a job I enjoy. And maybe I will be an independent developer who brings convenience and joy to other people.

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

My strengths and limitations pretty much remain the same as before. I believe I have the patience to absorb new concepts and tackle problems. When I get stuck, I do not usually suffer from negative feelings. Instead, I enjoy the process of looking for and trying out solutions. However, one limitation I did not mention is a lack of confidence. That might stop me from speaking up for myself or asking questions during Bootcamp.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

Perhaps working with other people. I don't know if I can make myself clear and understood during group work or presentations. Also, working with people online is a lot more harder than in real life, in my opinion.

What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

Probably all of them, communication, teamwork, and leadership skills. I want to become a good and confident communicator and team member who is comfortable with speaking their mind, working with different people, and just open communication in general.

What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

I would expect technical and emotional support and feedback on my performance from the team. On top of that, as a bonus, I hope the team can also provide some inside information in terms of getting into the IT industry and what it is like or share some experience if possible.

What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp

I would be self-disciplined, productive, and respectful during my learning journey. I'm not a morning person, so I will try to stick to the schedule and have enough rest. For the workload, I will try not to overwork and listen to my body when it gets tired and needs a break. And of course, provided the work on the day can be done on time. When I get stuck, I will give myself half an hour to find an answer. And if I can't, I will seek help from others. Most importantly, be open, respectful and honest with other learners, facilitators, industry/community representatives, and myself.
